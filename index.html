Never Stop Exploring

Why we do that ?

Passing the knowledge and inspiring the youth is one of our core values. We want the children to understand the beauty and the magic which represent water. Therefore, we are setting up several projects for this. We are giving speeches to schools, in front of classes. From first grade to senior year of highschool. We are also transparent with our functioning system. As soon as it is possible, we publish videos of our work, our studies with accessible (easily understood) explanations. Twice a year, we also welcome the public in our office to help them understand our purpose, to help them discovering what we are doing everyday and how we work. We love to believe that it creates vocations, it motivates them to help our planet. Additionally, every year, we accept students to do an observational internship. It is essential for children to become aware how lucky they are to live near the ocean. They must have the opportunity to discover a whole new world.

We are not only discovering the secrets that the ocean wants to tell us. We are also protecting it. In a world where pollution continues to increase, the nature is even more in danger. If it abandons us, it will drag us down too. Thus, we are carrying out studies about multiple subjects with the same theme : the undersea depths. We believe it is important to bring awareness, to wake up our consciousness about this. Going to the beach must not become a mirage, a construct of our mind. In our turn, we want to protect the sea and its treasures. Save it a sad fate.

Why exploring ? Exploration is a way to reconnect with the past. To try to understand what happened. It is an approach to discover history. We like to lead investigations, find a clue and trace an event. Unravelling the ocean, discovering every of its secrets, it’s one of our passions which animate us. There’s nothing more rewarding than finding a shipwreck after long weeks of searching and twist thinking. We investigate every single day with one goal : to understand. We want to comprehend what happened, we want to know how it happened. For us, it is important to highlight the sweet and sour secret stories of the sea. Bringing to light what some of us didn’t imagine. Like a testimony from the past, a warning message. The ocean is the king, and paid no heed to humans, when he feels like it, he takes back his rights and his power.
